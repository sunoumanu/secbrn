# BM25 and Lexical Search

BM25 is a bag-of-words ranking function that scores documents by term frequency and
inverse document frequency, with length normalisation. It is the workhorse of classic
keyword search and remains a strong baseline.

## Strengths and weaknesses

Lexical search nails exact terms, rare tokens, codes, and proper nouns that
[[Vector Search]] blurs together. Its weakness is vocabulary mismatch: it cannot match a
query to a passage that uses different words for the same idea. Combining BM25 with dense
retrieval in [[Hybrid Search]] gives the best of both, and the fused list is often passed
to [[Reranking]] for a final reorder.
